Red Wine Quality dataset 
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/uciml/red-wine-quality-cortez-et-al-2009

DESCRIPTION
Physicochemical measurements of Portuguese red wine (Vinho Verde) with expert-assigned quality ratings. Use case: predictive modeling of wine quality from chemical attributes for objective, scalable quality control.
Rows: 1599
Columns: 12


Data Dictionary
fixed acidity: 		Concentration of non-volatile acids (tartaric acid, etc.)
volatile acidity: 	Concentration of acetic acid; too much gives vinegar taste
citric acid: 		Concentration of citric acid; adds 'freshness' to wine
residual sugar: 	Amount of sugar left after fermentation (g/L)
chlorides: 		Salt content of the wine (g/L)
free sulfur dioxide: 	SO2 not bound to other molecules, protects against microbes (mg/L)
total sulfur dioxide: 	Total SO2; free + bound (mg/L)
density: 		Density of the wine (g/cm³); relates to sugar/alcohol content
pH: 			Measure of acidity (lower = more acidic)
sulphates: 		Potassium sulphate level; contributes to SO2 levels (g/L)
alcohol: 		Alcohol percentage by volume (%)
quality: 		Wine quality score (target variable; range 0–10, rated by tasters)
